Chapter 1 - PvP

PvP stands for player versus player, and it's one of the most important skills in Taming.io of all time. Either in a 1v1, or maybe more,
knowing the correct techniques is ideal. This chapter will be split into different sections, each explaining tactics for a certain weapon.

Section 1 - Prepearing and Tactics

Most idealy, you would want to have keybinds. Keybinds are very important for PvP, and help you make actions quicker. Even if you have used the standard 1 - 9 number
number keybinds for a long time, I would still recommend using keybinds. 1-9 number keybinds are fine, but not the most ideal and hard to preform some actions. I personally
recommend:

    Movement: WASD
    Food: 2
    Boostpad: E
    Spikes: F
    Primary Weapon: Space
    Secondary Weapon: Shift

With these keybinds, tactics are more simple. Some practices that you should try is spam healing while moving around, try quickly placing boost pads (defencive and offensive),
and one which is a bit more complicated than what I have shown so far, predicting a arrow's trajectory. This may seem a bit hard at first, but once you see the arrow being fired,
try juking back and avoid the arrow. Once you have mastered these techniques, the PvP guide should be easier to follow along.
